Do You Want To Give Your Life To Christ?

Please Follow The Steps Below.

"If we say we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us".

"He was delivered up for our trespasses, and raised for our justification"

If you declare with your mouth, “Jesus is Lord,” and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved. 10 For it is with your heart that you believe and are justified, and it is with your mouth that you profess your faith and are saved.

Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching.

Father, I am a sinner who needs a saviour. Thank you for giving Jesus to save me. I believe he died for my sin, and by his resurrection I received pardon. I confess Jesus as my Lord, and I turn from every way of life not pleasing to you, and walk in the light of the new life given me. Thank you Father, in Jesus name i have received.

AMEN!!
